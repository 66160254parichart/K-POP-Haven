Return Policy

1. Conditions for Returns

  • Products damaged during transit (e.g., dented box,
    broken items)
  • Wrong item received or does not match the order
  • Missing items or incomplete accessories

2. Non-returnable Products

  • Products that have been unsealed or used
  • Damage caused by the customer (e.g., tears, water damage)
  • Pre-order items, except in cases of seller error

3. Return Timeframe and Procedure

  • Request a return within 7 days, providing photos
    and proof of purchase.
  • Contact customer service via [LINE / Email / Phone].
  • Wait for evaluation within 3 business days.
  • Customer error: The customer covers return shipping costs.
  • Seller error: The seller covers return shipping costs.

4. Refund/Exchange Options

  • Exchange for a new product (if in stock).
  • Full refund (if the product is out of stock).
  • Refund processed within 7 business days.

5. Disclaimer

  • The store reserves the right to deny returns
    if conditions are not met.
