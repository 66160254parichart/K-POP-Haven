Shipping Information

1. Delivery Methods

  • Domestic Shipping: Uses private couriers (e.g., Kerry, J&T, Thailand Post) with a tracking number provided.
  • International Shipping: Standard services (e.g., EMS, DHL) depending on the destination and customer preferences.

2. Delivery Timeframes

  • Ready-to-Ship Products: Dispatched within 1-3 business days after payment confirmation.
  • Pre-order Products: Dispatched within 7-30 business days, depending on import timelines.

3. Shipping Fees

  • Calculated based on weight and destination (details shown during checkout).
  • Free shipping for orders that meet the minimum purchase amount (as per promotions).

4. Tracking Your Order

  • After dispatch, a tracking number will be sent via [Email/LINE/SMS].
  • Order status can be tracked on the courier's website.
